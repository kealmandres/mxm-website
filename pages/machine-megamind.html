FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What exactly are "Bespoke Bot Battalions" and AI agents?

+

Think of AI agents as specialized digital team members, custom-built to perform specific tasks. A "Bespoke Bot Battalion" is a coordinated team of these agents working together within an "agentic workflow" to handle complex processes. Unlike general AI tools, these are tailored precisely to your needs and integrated into your systems - trained by you, through us.

How is this different from using standard AI chatbots?

+

While standard chatbots are powerful general tools, our custom agents are:

  • Tailored: Built for your unique business processes.
  • Integrated: Designed to work seamlessly within your existing software stack.
  • Autonomous: Capable of performing multi-step tasks and collaborating with other agents.
  • Context-Aware: Trained on your specific business knowledge for more relevant outputs.

What information do you need during the "Architect" phase?

+

The Architect phase is crucial for understanding your needs. We typically need insights into your business goals, current workflows, specific tasks you want to automate or enhance, existing software systems, and any relevant; documentation, data, or knowledge bases that agents can learn from - you might commonly hear the word “context” when training agents, which is simply the aforementioned relevant resources. This collaborative process ensures the agents we build are perfectly aligned with your operational reality and strategic objectives. We commonly detect contexts that clients would never have thought of to train agents on, this is crucial to the success of the agent.

Can agents integrate with our current tools?

+

Yes, integration is a key part of our process. During the Architect and Build phases, we focus on ensuring the agents can interact with your existing systems (like CRMs, project management tools, databases, communication platforms, etc.) where possible and beneficial. We aim to create a seamless Human+AI partnership, augmenting your current operations rather than requiring a complete overhaul. An added benefit is that some clients walk away with a leaner tech stack due to the capabilities of their new agents.
