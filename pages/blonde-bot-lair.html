If you asked AI to generate a concoction of Oscar Wilde, Hedy Lamarr, Don Draper, Paris Hilton, Joan Rivers and a sprinkle of Steve Jobs… you would get me.

I have a passion for leading businesses owners to the cutting edge of Artificial Intelligence. While I have been running businesses since 2016, establishing a marketing agency in the midst of a global pandemic, epitomises my approach to life - diamonds are made under pressure.

My love for marketing began when I was offered a position at Marriott International's Digital Marketing Hub for Southern Europe. Upon relocating overseas for this position, I found the industry a perfect fit. I absolutely adored my team as well as all the phenomenal work that came with the territory. Until 2020 rolled around, taking a sledgehammer to tourism.

Choosing the path of self-employment once again, I opened up my own Digital Marketing and Media Agency in February of 2021... with no idea a four month lockdown was just around the corner - it's been an exponential climb ever since, for the business and my cortisol.

To date, I have also been Chief of Staff for several businesses & founders in tandem with running the agency. With a clear adrenaline addiction - I will undoubtedly unpack with a shrink in my 30's - in 2023, I decided to cannibalise my own business as my love affair with AI became more than just a fling.
